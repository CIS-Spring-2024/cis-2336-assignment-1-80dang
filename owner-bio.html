Meet the Owner: Annie Dang

A Culinary Journey with Heart and Favor

Annie Dang, the heart and soul behind UOH Sugarland Cafeteria, has woven her passion for food into the very fabric of this beloved establishment. Her journey began in the bustling streets of Saigon, where she learned the art of flavors, spices, and community from her grandmother's kitchen.

The Art of Balance

Annies commitment to quality and balance is evident in every plate. From hearty comfort foods to vibrant salads, she curates a menu that caters to diverse tastes. But it is not just about the food—it is about the experience. Annies warm smile greets every customer, turning strangers into regulars.

A Message from Annie

“To our patrons, thank you for sharing your stories, laughter, and meals with us. UOH Sugarland Cafeteria is more than a business—it is a family. Come hungry, leave fulfilled.”
